Privacy Policy for WordGriddle

Last Updated: March 2025

Thank you for playing WordGriddle! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our game on our website. Please take a moment to read through this policy to understand our practices.

1. Information We Collect

When you play WordGriddle, we use your browser's localStorage feature to save your game progress locally on your device. This may include:

  • Which puzzles you have completed or started.
  • Your current progress in individual puzzles.
  • Any in-game settings or preferences you have selected.

We do not collect any personal information (such as your name, email address, or location) unless you explicitly provide it to us (e.g., through a contact form or newsletter sign-up).

2. How We Use Your Information

The information stored in localStorage is used solely to enhance your gaming experience by:

  • Retaining your progress so you can continue where you left off.
  • Saving your preferences to personalize your gameplay.
  • Ensuring the game functions as intended.

We do not share, sell, or transmit this information to third parties.

3. Storage and Security

All game progress and preferences are stored locally on your device using localStorage. This data remains on your device and is not transferred to our servers or any external systems.

Please note that:

  • You can clear your localStorage data at any time through your browser settings. Doing so will reset your game progress.
  • localStorage is subject to your browser's storage limits (typically around 5MB per domain).

4. Your Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Clearing Data: You can delete your game progress at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for our website.
  • Disabling localStorage: If you prefer not to use localStorage, you can disable it in your browser settings. However, this may prevent the game from saving your progress or preferences.

5. Third-Party Services

Our game does not use third-party services that collect or process your data. However, if we add such services in the future (e.g., analytics or ads), we will update this Privacy Policy to reflect those changes.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we handle your information.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through the game's Discord or Reddit presence or by using contact information given by us when you were provided access to this website.

By using WordGriddle, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
